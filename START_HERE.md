## 🔐 Google Sheets Integration

Your contact form can submit to Google Sheets in 3 ways:

1. **Google Apps Script** (Recommended)
   - Direct Google Sheets integration
   - Most control
   - ~10 minutes setup

2. **Formspree** (Easiest)
   - No coding required
   - Email notifications
   - ~3 minutes setup

3. **Netlify Forms** (If using Netlify)
   - Works with Netlify deployment
   - Built-in spam protection
   - ~5 minutes setup

**See:** GOOGLE_SHEETS_INTEGRATION.md for detailed steps
